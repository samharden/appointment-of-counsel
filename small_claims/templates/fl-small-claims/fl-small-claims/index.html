Baker Act:

The Baker Act is a civil commitment process for people who are a danger to themselves or others due to mental illness. The first step to having a person evaluated under the Baker Act is to complete and file a Baker Act petition in the court for the county where the person lives or resides.

Marchman Act:

The Marchman Act is a civil commitment process for people who are a danger to themselves or others due to substance abuse or addiction. The first step to having a person evaluated under the Marchman Act is to complete and file a Marchman Act petition in the court for the county where the person lives or resides.
