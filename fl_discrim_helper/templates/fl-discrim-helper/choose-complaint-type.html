Welcome to the Florida Discrimination Complaint Assistant. The Assistant is designed to guide you through the steps of determining if you have a potential discrimination claim, and filing that claim with the Florida Commission on Human Relations.

Public Accomodation

If you've been denied service at a restaurant, hotel, bar, theater, gas station, store, or any other kind of business that accomodates the public because of your race, sex, a disability, marital status, national origin, or various other reasons, you may be the victim of Public Accomodation discrimination.

Employment

If you were fired, denied a job, or turned down for a promotion because of your race, sex, a disability, marital status, national origin, or various other reasons, you may be the victim of Employment discrimination.

Housing

If you were evicted or denied housing because of your race, sex, a disability, marital status, national origin, or various other reasons, you may be the victim of Employment discrimination.
